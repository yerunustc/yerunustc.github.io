叶 润 (Run YE)



 

     
个人简介

	    Run YE is a lecturer at Department of Physics, Yancheng Teachers University. He got his PhD major in nuclear and particle 
	    physics at University of Science and Technology of China, under the supervision of Prof. Bangjiao Ye. His current
	    research interests are focused on positron annihilation spectroscopy (PAS) and its application in the study of defects in 
	    diluted magnetic semiconductors and nanomaterials.
